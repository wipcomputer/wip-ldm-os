Privacy Policy

Kaleidoscope Demo

Last updated: April 2, 2026

WIP Computer, Inc. ("we", "us") operates the Kaleidoscope demo at wip.computer/demo.

What We Collect

  • Passkey credential ID and public key (for authentication)
  • Username (optional, if you provide one)
  • Wallet balance (simulated, for demo purposes)

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Email addresses (unless voluntarily provided later)
  • Passwords (passkeys only, no passwords exist)
  • Biometric data (Face ID / fingerprint verification happens on your device, we never receive biometric data)
  • Photos (camera captures are processed in your browser, never uploaded)
  • Browsing history or tracking data
  • Cookies (we use sessionStorage only, cleared when you close the browser)

How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication: verifying your passkey when you sign in
  • Demo functionality: tracking simulated wallet balance
  • Product improvement: aggregate usage patterns (no personal data)

Third-Party Services

  • xAI (Grok Imagine API): receives text prompts for image generation. Does not receive your photos or personal data.
  • No analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No data brokers

Data Storage

Your passkey data is stored on our server at wip.computer. It is not encrypted at rest in this demo version. Production versions will implement end-to-end encryption where we architecturally cannot read your data.

Data Deletion

Contact hello@wip.computer to request deletion of your passkey data.

Your Rights

You can delete your passkey from your device at any time through your device settings (Settings > Passwords on iOS, chrome://settings/passwords on Chrome).

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.
